MoneyMate Privacy Policy

Effective date: 24 April 2026 Version: 1.0 DPO contact: privacy@moneymate.app

1. Introduction

MoneyMate is a personal expense-tracking iOS application operated by the MoneyMate team ("we", "us"). This policy explains what personal data we collect when you use the app, why we collect it, and the rights you have over it.

Using MoneyMate constitutes agreement to this policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the app.

For any privacy question or complaint, contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) at privacy@moneymate.app. We respond within 7 business days.

2. Data we collect

MoneyMate only collects what's necessary to provide the expense-tracking service:

  • Transactions — date, amount, category, notes, receipt images (only when you attach one)
  • Categories — expense/income categories you create or use from defaults
  • Budgets — per-category spending limits + alert thresholds you set
  • User profile — internal ID (Firebase UID), display name, phone number or email (depending on which sign-in method you choose: Phone OTP, Google Sign-In, or Sign in with Apple), avatar (optional)
  • App settings — currency, notification preferences, onboarding state
  • Analytics — aggregate product-interaction events, containing no PII (see Story 10.8). You can opt out any time in Settings → Privacy.
  • Crash logs (Crashlytics) — stack traces captured when the app crashes. No financial data or PII (see Story 10.7).

Data we do NOT collect

  • Location
  • Contacts, calendars, or photos outside what you explicitly select
  • Web browsing activity, advertising cookies
  • Biometric data (Face ID / Touch ID runs entirely on-device by iOS — we never receive it)

3. How we use your data

Data is used only for:

  1. Providing the service — showing your balance, reports, budget suggestions, syncing between your devices
  2. Improving the product — aggregate analytics help us identify which features need work
  3. Security — fraud detection, anomalous sign-in
  4. Support — when you actively contact the DPO

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

4. Storage and security

4.1. Where data is stored

  • Cloud (Firebase Firestore) — asia-southeast1 region (Singapore), closest to Vietnam for latency
  • Cloud Storage (Firebase Storage) — same region, for receipt images
  • Your device — SwiftData cache + UserDefaults, protected by iOS Data Protection at completeUntilFirstUserAuthentication level

4.2. Encryption

  • At rest — AES-256 managed by Google Cloud
  • In transit — TLS 1.2 or newer
  • On device — iOS Data Protection auto-encrypts when the device is locked

4.3. Access control

  • Firestore Security Rules ensure only you (by Firebase UID) can read/write your data
  • MoneyMate staff do NOT have access to your personal financial data; anonymous debug access requires your consent

5. Third-party sharing

We use the following services under Data Processing Agreements:

Service Purpose Data shared
Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage, Cloud Messaging, Analytics, Crashlytics) Backend & infrastructure All account data + financial data + analytics + crash logs
Google Sign-In Authentication (optional) Email + display name you authorize
Apple Sign in with Apple Authentication (optional) Email (can be hidden) + name you authorize

We do NOT share with any advertising partner, data broker, or other third party.

6. Your rights (PDPD — Vietnam Decree 13/2023, plus GDPR-equivalent)

You have full rights and can exercise them directly in the app:

Right How
Right to be informed Read this policy any time in Settings → Privacy Policy
Right to access Settings → Export data to CSV (Story 9.2) — download all your transactions
Right to rectification Edit or delete any transaction, category, or budget any time
Right to erasure Settings → Delete account — permanent deletion within 30 days (Story 1.6)
Right to withdraw analytics consent Settings → Privacy → Turn off analytics (Story 10.8)
Right to complain Email privacy@moneymate.app — we respond within 7 business days

6.1. Account-deletion process

When you delete your account:

  1. Immediately: account is disabled, you can no longer sign in
  2. Within 30 days: all transactions, categories, budgets, receipt images, and profile data are permanently removed from Firebase
  3. Already-anonymized aggregate analytics are retained (cannot be linked back to you)
  4. Anonymous Crashlytics logs are kept for up to 90 days per Google's policy

7. Push notifications

If you enable notifications, we send:

  • Daily expense-logging reminder (default 8 PM)
  • Budget alerts when spending reaches 80% / 100%

You can disable them any time in Settings → Notifications. No ads, no marketing messages.

8. Children

MoneyMate is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you discover a child under 13 has registered, please contact the DPO so we can delete the account immediately.

9. Changes to this policy

When we update the policy:

  • Material changes (expanding data collection, changing purposes): we notify you in-app + by email (if available) at least 30 days before the effective date
  • Minor changes (copy edits, contact updates): we bump the version number on this page

Change history appears at the bottom of this document.

10. Legal compliance

MoneyMate complies with:

  • Decree 13/2023/NĐ-CP (Vietnam) — Personal Data Protection
  • GDPR (EU) — to the extent applicable to EU users
  • Apple App Store Guidelines — App Tracking Transparency, App Privacy Details

If compelled by Vietnamese legal authorities, we may have to disclose data following due legal process. We will notify you when legally permitted.
